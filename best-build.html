🛡️ How to Obtain & Master Haki

The Haki Quest:

To unlock Haki, you must travel to Lawless Island and find the Haki Master NPC. He will provide the initial quest requiring you to defeat powerful island guardians.

Training Mechanics:

Once unlocked, Haki levels up through usage and combat. Killing high-level NPCs on Lawless Island or Tower Island is the fastest way to hit Lvl 100 Mastery.

Haki Power Spikes:

  • Armament (Busoshoku): Grants physical hardening. Mandatory for damaging Logia-type users and boosting Ice Queen Sword DPS.
  • Observation (Kenbunshoku): Unlocks the "Dodge" bar. Higher levels increase the number of auto-dodges and expand your sensory range.
  • Conqueror's (Haoshoku): A rare power-up that adds a secondary damage multiplier to all attacks, including the Blizzard move.
WARNING: Observation Haki training requires you to successfully dodge attacks repeatedly. Do not AFK while training this!

🚀 Character Ascension: The A10 Journey

Ascension is the process of breaking your character's limits. To reach the peak of the Ice Meta, hitting Ascension 10 (A10) is mandatory. This process resets your level but grants permanent, multiplicative power boosts that are required for end-game content.

The Level Cap:

You must reach Level 13,000 to attempt the final A10 Ascension. Farming at Tower Island is the most efficient way to grind this XP.

Damage Milestone:

Before ascending to A10, your build must be capable of dealing at least 7,500B (7.5 Trillion) Total Damage to pass the final trial NPC.

A10 Permanent Buffs:

+500% Base DMG +250% Base HP +50% Speed
Note: Every Ascension rank increases the cost of your Stat Rerolls. Secure your SSS Rank early!
📍 Strategic Farming & Grind Spots

To reach the Level 13,000 ceiling, you cannot farm randomly. You must utilize these specific islands to maximize your time-to-power ratio.

Tower Island:

Primary Goal: Infinite Tower progression.
Key Drops: Primordial Runes and massive XP chunks. This is the only place to test your Blizzard move's 100B+ damage against scaling enemies.

Lawless Island:

Primary Goal: Haki Mastery & Artifacts.
Key Drops: Abyssal Crown and High-Tier Haki XP. The NPC density here allows for the fastest Armament and Observation training.

Sailor Island (The Hub):

Used primarily for Trait Rerolls (Celestial) and Race Rerolls (Swordblessed). Keep your gems saved for these two specific gacha mechanics.

⚔️ Combat Mastery: The Blizzard Loop

To maximize your 100B+ DPS, you must abuse the synergy between your Celestial Trait and the Ice Queen Sword. This rotation ensures constant pressure and infinite crowd control.

1. Ice Shard Initiation

Start by chipping away at the boss's shield. This move triggers your Frostbane Clan damage scaling early.

2. THE BLIZZARD (V-Key)

Your primary nuke. Thanks to the -30% CDR from Celestial, the downtime is minimal. This move hits multiple times, proccing lifesteal from the Swordblessed Race.

THE CDR ADVANTAGE:

Normal players wait 10+ seconds for a reset. With this meta build, your Blizzard reset is nearly instant, allowing you to stack the 1.25x Damage Cap from Frostbane within the first 5 seconds of combat.

Rotation: Initiate -> Scale -> Blizzard Spam -> Repeat
❄️ Boss Guide: Defeating the Ice Queen

The Ice Queen Boss is the gatekeeper of this meta. She is located at the peak of the Frozen North Island. Defeating her is the only way to obtain the Sword and the Frost Empress Title.

How to Spawn:

She spawns every 2 hours server-time, or you can force-spawn her using a Glacial Key (purchasable at the Frozen Shop for 50M Beli).

Her Deadly Moves:

Absolute Zero: A stun move that freezes you for 3 seconds. Use your Observation Haki to auto-dodge this, or your run will end.

💎 Efficient Gem Farming

Since obtaining the Celestial Trait and Swordblessed Race requires thousands of Gems, you need a high-speed farming method. Here are the two meta-approved ways to stock up.

Method 1: AFK Cave

Located on Starter Island. While the gems per minute are lower, using an auto-clicker with your Ice Queen Sword's area-of-effect moves ensures a steady flow of gems while you sleep.

Method 2: Boss Rush

The fastest active method. Defeating the Ice Queen or Panda Sorcerer repeatedly rewards huge Gem chunks. With your 100B+ damage, you can clear these bosses in seconds.

Spending Priority:

  1. Race Reroll: Secure Swordblessed first for the multiplier.
  2. Trait Reroll: Aim for Celestial (0.1% chance) to unlock the cooldown reduction.
  3. Stat Reroll: Final polish to hit SSS Rank.
🐼 Boss Guide: The Panda Sorcerer

The Panda Sorcerer is the most contested boss for end-game players. He is the only source of Adamantite, the material required to reach the legendary B10 Blessing status at Shibuya Station.

Location:

Found hidden within the Bamboo Forest on the outskirts of Shibuya. He has a fast respawn timer but incredibly high HP.

Survival Tip:

His "Cursed Pulse" attack can bypass shields. Maintain your distance and use the Blizzard move's maximum range to stay safe.

To reach the absolute peak of the Ice Meta, your outfit and sword aren't enough. You need to equip specific accessories that provide hidden multipliers to your Sword and Melee stats.

🏰 End-Game: Infinite Tower Progression

The Infinite Tower on Tower Island is where your 100B+ Blizzard damage is truly tested. As you climb higher, enemies gain massive HP pools that only a perfect Ice Meta Build can shred.

Floors 1 - 25:

Warm-up stages. Your Celestial Trait cooldown reduction allows you to clear these floors in under 5 minutes.

Floors 26 - 50+:

This is where Primordial Runes drop. Enemies here have trillions of HP. Without your B10 Shibuya Blessing, you will struggle to move past Floor 40.

TOWER REWARDS:

  • Rune Fragments: Used to level up your Primordial Rune to Lvl 60.
  • Ascension Essence: Needed for the jump from A9 to A10.
  • Legendary Gems: Higher drop rates than the AFK Cave.
